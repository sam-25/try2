About us

Tea Kettle

Tea Kettle® is a sincere attempt to offer you, our discerning customer, some healthier options to choose from. Our range of Cookies and Kharis has been carefully handcrafted to cater to your taste buds and health as well. We, a team of ex-bankers, established Tea Kettle® in 2016 with an aim of offering Health, Taste and Quality to our B2B customers for all their corporate High Tea requirements.
